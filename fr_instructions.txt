Instructions

1. Heat oil on medium heat with spices (minus garlic)
2. Add chopped veggies
3. Once veggies are cooked to desired texture, add eggs
4. Once eggs are cooked, turn down heat to medium-low
5. Add butter and garlic 
6. Add frozen rice and quinoa
7. Turn up heat to medium-high and stir continually until rice is hot
8. Add soy sauce to taste
9. Cook for 2 more minutes 
10. Remove from heat and serve
